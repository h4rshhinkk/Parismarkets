Myfxbook - FAQ

What is Myfxbook used for?

Myfxbook is a completely free automated online trading system that provides traders to monitor, analyse, discuss, and evaluate their trading methods and account performance. Myfxbook connects to your trading platform and imports all previous and current activities from your brokerage account automatically.

How Can I Get Myfxbook?

To begin, navigate to Myfxbook and register for an account. Myfxbook's homepage features a convenient 'Quick Signup' button to the right. Create a unique Username for yourself, enter your Email address, and establish a password. After that, complete the Captcha, agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Statement, and click Sign Me Up..

How Do I Connect My MT4 To Myfxbook?

When the Myfxbook tool is connected to your trading platform, it instantly imports all of your previous and current transactions from your trading account. It is imperative to choose a fully regulated broker such as Paris Markets, which has excellent customer support to help with any problems.

Is My Account Info Safe with Myfxbook?

Myfxbook is generally considered safe but with any online activity, there is always a risk.

How Do I Copy Trades on Myfxbook?

Copy trading enables you to simply copy the positions of another trader. You choose your investment amount and just replicate what they do in real-time — whenever that trader makes a deal, your account will make the same move.
