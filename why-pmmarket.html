Trusted Global CFD and Forex Broker

Paris Markets is your one-stop destination to trade CFDs across Forex, Shares, Indices, Commodities & Cryptocurrencies.

Our range of trading accounts offer you consistently tighter spreads, starting from as low as 0.0 pips. We’ve partnered with leading banking and non-banking financial institutions to provide a deep liquidity pool, offering among the best available market prices.

Access over 10,000 tradeable CFD products on the world’s biggest exchanges. Choose from our powerful platform options including MT4, MT5 and Iress and take your trading with you via our mobile, app and web based accessibility options.

Raw Forex Pricing on MT4

Trade Forex on streamed directly from our top tier liquidity providers.

  • Deep liquidity

    • Benefit from a large liquidity pool of leading, tier one, regulated financial institutions for a diverse liquidity mix and aggressive pricing

  • Transparency

    • Stay in control with transparent pricing and commissions with a trading account from Paris Markets, a broker.

  • Low latency

    • Minimize slippage and experience ultra-low latency order execution, using best-in-class technology

  • Tight Raw Spreads

    • Our pricing model fosters price competition, leading to tight bid/ask spreads. Our spreads regularly go as low as 0.0 pips on the major currency pairs

Direct Market Access Pricing on IRESS

Trade Direct Market Access (DMA) Share CFDs via our powerful
Iress DMA Trading Platform

  • Transparency

    • With an independent exchange price feed you can see every bid and every offer for the security you are trading

  • Become a price maker, not a price taker

    • By passing 100% of DMA orders into the live market, Paris Markets allows you to have an effect on the demand and supply of the product you are trading

  • Increased liquidity

    • A DMA model allows the trader to access identical market liquidity to the underlying market
