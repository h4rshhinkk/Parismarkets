Open Forex Trading Account

Paris Markets is an Australian CFD and forex broker founded in 2005. We understand that no two traders are the same. As a result, we offer a variety of account types for forex and CFD trading. We pride ourselves on having tight spreads, flexible leverage and lightning-fast execution, but it is our variety and customer support which set us apart.



You can open an MT4 or MT5 trading account with as little as $100 . Access 60+ currency pairs, indices, commodities and cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin with a maximum leverage up to 500:1. Trade major currency pairs such as the EUR/USD, USD/JPY and GBP/USD among other heavily traded currencies including the Australian Dollar (AUD) and Hong Kong Dollar (HKD). Our android and iOS mobile apps allow you to trade on the go with several funding options available including credit card, debit card, Neteller, Skrill, BPAY, Poli and PayPal.

Start trading share CFDs, future CFDs, forex and commodities across 7 global exchanges including the Australian Stock Exchange (ASX) and New York Stock Exchange (NYSE).
